Sardine Patties

Description

Canned sardines, particularly one in tomato sauce, are a vital part of Filipino diet when the budget is tight and one cannot afford to buy fresh fish or meat. Along with instant ramen noodles, canned sardines can feed an entire family or individual. They are inexpensive and convenient, that is why it has become one of the most preferred food supplies for college students back in my home country.

Ingredients

  • 1 canned sardines in tomato sauce (7.5 oz)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Pinch of ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Open canned sardines and drain all the sauce. Transfer sardines to a medium bowl and mash with a fork.
  2. Add the eggs, flour, salt, pepper and mix thoroughly.
    3. Heat oil in a skillet. Drop mixture by the spoonful, then flatten and fry, roughly about 30 seconds on each side.
  3. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  4. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce or eat as it is.
